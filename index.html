information

The Swift Knights is an RPG inspired by and based on the TS Cinematic Universe and the 10 Taylor Swift eras. Each character is based on a different era. Progression is straight forward; each knight is ranked according to their stats from descending to ascending order. To progress from one knight to another, players must complete a certain XP Requirement from completing quests.
